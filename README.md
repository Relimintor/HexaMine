# HexaMine
Hexamine is a 3d voxel game, like minecraft but theres 2 key differences, 1 its not blocks, its hexagons (so its gonna be 6 sides from the side up, but still be connected to the ground) 2 its not infinite world genertion, instead the world is a planet, if i walk to the other side im gonna come to the same point i started from just by walking in a line

The hotbar is gonna be hexagons just like everything else, right now the plan isi it to be a web game, THO all heavy logic will be handled by c++ files, and compiled with wasm, so it works in a web enviroment

When booting up, its gonna be the picture of the header file, while the background fades in, and then the header will ove to the top of th screen, and the stuff like new world, load world, multiplayer, options, quit (it closes the tab or something), with at the bottom the current version.
when clicking new world, a screen will come up where you can choose btw creative and survival, and choose the world name, choose a seed (almost always random if you dont put anything in it), and you can choose how large the "planet" will be with these options: tiny, medium, large, collosal, and under that have the options of earth like and super flat, beside it is a white rotating planet that shows how the terrain might look (so u see the differene btw super flat and earth like) and its size based on what you click, with a button under that says create, the worlds will be saved using the cookies we will be using, so you can play it again later, and if it fails you can download the world as a file and everything on it will be saved,
